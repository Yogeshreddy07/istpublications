About the Journal

Innovative Science and Technologies is a peer-reviewed, fully open access, multidisciplinary journal covering engineering, technology, and science. It publishes both original research and review articles.

Scope of ISTP

The scope of the ISTP is to provide an idea of research, academic related important references with dissemination of research outcomes. This supports high-level learning, teaching and research in the fields of Engineering, Science and Technology.

Our Aim

The aim of the journal to promote the new Innovative ideas in all fields of Engineering and Technology as one platform so that if anybody wants to integrate their ideas with other field of Computer Science.
